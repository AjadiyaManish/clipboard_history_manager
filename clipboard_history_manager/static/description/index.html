Workflow Showcase
Clipboard History & Bookmarks
From installation to one-click re-use — your clipboard, always under control.
1
Install the Module
Search Clipboard History Manager in Odoo Apps and click Module Info to install. No dependencies required.
2
Clipboard History Panel
Click the Clipboard icon in top bar. The History tab shows all copied items with timestamps. Bookmarked items are highlighted with a ★ star.
3
View Clipboard History
The History tab tooltip confirms "View clipboard history". All captured items listed newest-first with precise copy timestamps for full traceability.
4
Smart Search & Filter
Type in the search bar to instantly filter history. Shows a result count (e.g. "3 results found") — retrieval is instant even with large histories.
5
1-Click Re-Copy
Hover any history item to reveal action buttons. Click the Copy icon to instantly put that text back on your clipboard — ready to paste anywhere in Odoo.
6
Bookmark & Remove Bookmark
Click the ★ Star button to bookmark any item. Tooltip shows "Remove bookmark" when active — toggling is instant and persistent across sessions.
7
Delete Items
Click the 🗑️ Delete icon to remove any entry from history permanently. Keep your clipboard list clean and relevant at all times.
8
Bookmarks Tab
Switch to Bookmarks tab for a dedicated view of all starred items only — your personal shortlist of frequently reused text, always at hand.
9
Settings & Premium Features
The Settings tab lets you enable/disable Clipboard Manager and Notifications toggles. Premium: auto-capture, bookmark management, smart search, copy frequency tracking, customizable notifications.
Key Feature
👤 User-Wise Persistent History
Clipboard history is saved per user — logout and login again, your history is always there.
✦   How It Works
Every clipboard item is stored in Odoo's database linked to the logged-in user's ID. When the user logs out and logs back in — even from a different browser or device — their complete clipboard history and bookmarks are fully restored automatically.
 
🔐 User A logs out
History saved to DB under User A's ID
 
✅ User A logs back in
Full history & bookmarks instantly restored
 
🔒 User B logs in
Sees only their own history — fully isolated
💾
Persistent Storage
History stored in Odoo DB — survives logout, browser close, and session expiry.
👤
Per-User Isolation
Each user's clipboard is private. Multi-user environments fully supported.
🌎
Cross-Device Access
Login from any browser or device — history instantly available, no sync needed.
